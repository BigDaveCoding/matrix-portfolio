About Me

I began my journey into software development with a curiosity for video games, creating simple games in Unity without much prior knowledge. Initially, I had no idea how to code, so I decided to dive into the world of programming through a little self-research. After some Googling, I stumbled upon The Odin Project and completed their foundations course, which gave me a solid understanding of web development. Eager to build on my skills, I then explored data analytics & computer science through Codecademy, further honing my technical abilities.
Today, I'm pushing myself even further by enrolling in iO Academy's intensive 12-week Software Developer course, where I continue to expand my coding knowledge and experience.
